## UNITY IMPORT SETTINGS

After downloading from Midjourney:
1. **Remove background** if needed (use remove.bg or Photoshop)
2. **Resize** to exact dimensions (64x64, 32x32, or 16x16)
3. **Save as PNG** with transparency
4. **Import to Unity**, then in Inspector:
   - Texture Type: `Sprite (2D and UI)`
   - Sprite Mode: `Single`
   - Pixels Per Unit: `64` (for 64x64) or `32` (for 32x32) or `16` (for 16x16)
   - Filter Mode: `Point (no filter)`
   - Compression: `None`
5. Click **Apply**
